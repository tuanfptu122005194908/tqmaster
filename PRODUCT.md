# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Vietnamese university students (ages 18-25) buying study materials and practicing mock exams on their phones and laptops. They're stressed before exam season, looking for quick access to reliable materials. Secondary users: admins managing content, orders, and users from a desktop dashboard.

## Product Purpose

A digital learning marketplace where students purchase subject-specific study packs (theory content + mock exams) organized by academic semester (kỳ 1-9). Success means a student can go from landing to studying in under 3 minutes: browse by term, buy, and immediately start a timed mock exam or read theory.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, focused, approachable. Not flashy or gamified. The platform should feel like a well-organized study room: calm, purposeful, efficient. Students should feel confident, not overwhelmed.

## Anti-references

- Udemy-style crowded layouts with aggressive upsell banners
- Duolingo-style gamified streaks and animations
- Generic Bootstrap admin templates
- Dark academic aesthetics that feel heavy for a study tool

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity under pressure.** Students use this when stressed. Every action must be obvious; never make them wonder where to click.
2. **Semester-first organization.** The primary wayfinding is by semester (kỳ), not by subject alphabetically. Mirror how students think about their coursework.
3. **Exam mode is sacred.** The timed exam experience demands full focus. Remove nav chrome, surface only the question and controls, and make the timer visible without being anxiety-inducing.
4. **Admin density is a feature.** Admins need to move fast through hundreds of orders and questions. Dense tables with clear affordances serve them better than spacious cards.
5. **Trust signals matter.** Payment via bank transfer requires trust. Show clear bank info, clean order tracking, and honest status communication.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Vietnamese primary language (UI copy in Vietnamese). Mobile-first responsive layout for students on phones.
